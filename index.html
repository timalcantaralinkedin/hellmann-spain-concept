Welcome to Hellmann Spain

Hellmann Worldwide Logistics established its Spanish country organisation in 1999. From four locations in Barcelona, Madrid, Bilbao and Valencia, our 160 local specialists provide end-to-end logistics services for inbound, outbound and domestic shipments across the Iberian Peninsula.

Our offices are positioned along Spain's principal trade corridors. The Mediterranean container ports of Barcelona and Valencia handle our seafreight volumes, Madrid Barajas anchors our airfreight operations, and Bilbao serves the Atlantic industrial corridor that connects Northern Spain and Portugal to the European hinterland.

As part of a family-owned group with more than 150 years of history and over 12,000 employees in 62 countries, Hellmann Spain combines local decision-making with the operational depth of a global network.

Spotlight

The Iberia and Latin America corridor.

Spain's shared language with Latin America and its long-standing commercial ties make it the European point of entry for many transatlantic supply chains. Hellmann Spain operates a dedicated desk to manage shipments between Iberian ports and the Hellmann offices located in 16 Latin American countries.

  • Weekly FCL and LCL departures from Valencia and Algeciras to Mexico, Brazil, Chile and Argentina.
  • Spanish-language customer service from Madrid, aligned with Latin American business hours.
  • Customs and trade-compliance expertise covering AEO certification, EUR.1 movement certificates and EUR-MED preferential origin documentation.
  • Origin-to-destination handover through Hellmann offices across 16 Latin American countries.
