Welcome to Hellmann Spain

Local roots. Global reach. Built for Iberia.

Since 1999, Hellmann has been building Spain's logistics network from the ground up — placing offices where industry actually lives. Today, our country team connects the manufacturing corridors of Catalonia and the Basque Country, the fashion capitals of Madrid and Barcelona, and the renewable energy hubs of Valencia and Andalusia with the rest of the world.

Spain isn't a single market — it's four economies in one country, each with its own port, its own industry mix, and its own rhythm. Our four locations are positioned to match that reality, not paper over it.

We're a family-owned forwarder operating in a market dominated by global giants — and we like it that way. Local decisions, faster answers, and a 150-year company history behind every shipment.

Our products in Spain

Four modes. One coordinated network.

End-to-end coverage across air, sea, road and contract logistics — orchestrated from a single Spanish account team.

Air Freight

Daily consolidations through Madrid Barajas and Barcelona El Prat. Time-critical capacity to the Americas, Asia, and intra-Europe — including charter solutions for project cargo.

Learn more →

Sea Freight

FCL and LCL services from Spain's busiest Mediterranean ports — Valencia, Barcelona, Algeciras — and Atlantic hubs at Bilbao. Direct services to LATAM, North Africa, and the Far East.

Learn more →

Road Freight

Full-load and groupage across Iberia and into Europe. Daily departures linking Madrid, Catalonia, the Basque Country, Valencia, and Portugal to Hellmann's European network.

Contract Logistics

Warehousing, value-added services and fulfilment from strategic locations in Madrid and Barcelona. Built for fashion, consumer goods, and high-mix manufacturing.

Spotlight · Iberia ↔ LATAM corridor

Europe's natural gateway to Latin America runs through Spain.

Shared language, deep historical trade ties, and the densest direct shipping and air-cargo schedules between Europe and LATAM all land at Spanish ports and airports first. We've built a dedicated desk to make that advantage usable.

  • Weekly FCL/LCL services from Valencia and Algeciras to Mexico, Brazil, Chile, and Argentina.
  • Spanish-language customer service from Madrid, mirroring time zones for Mexico and South America.
  • Customs and trade compliance expertise for AEO, EUR.1, and EUR-MED preferential origin.
  • Local-to-local handoff through Hellmann's offices across 16 LATAM countries.
